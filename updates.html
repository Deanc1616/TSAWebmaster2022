News Updates







Take aim for the sand pit or the mat, because jumping events are coming soon to Fun Run Friends and Family Virtual Races!



We're all track and field athletes, but what's one thing we haven't introduced yet? Field events! So, starting very soon, we will be offering many different field events to cater to many other types of athletes, and to make the community much more inclusive in our efforts. While we haven't got the logistics down completely yet, we're sure that soon enough, we'll see our strongest jumpers will come out to show off and have a great time

We Will Offer:

  • Long Jump
  • High Jump
  • And Triple Jump!

    • Thank you to everyone who made this possible, as adding these jumping and other field events will bring so many more athletes together!

    Hurdle events coming to Fun Run Friends and Family Virtual Races!



    In light of the recent adding of the field events listed below, we have decided also to make a shift to doing a few track and field hurdling races that people will really enjoy. From sprinting all the way to a distance event, these are a lot harder to set up for yourself, but with the help of others, can be a fun event for all! But be warned, some of these hurdles are tough!



    We Offer:

    • 100 meter hurdles
    • 110 meter hurdles(Olympic Event rather than the 100m hurdles)
    • 400 meter hurdles (also known as the intermediate hurdles)


    We'll leave you to it! Best of luck and can't wait to see what everyone is capable of!

    Get Ready For Virtual Track and Field Races!



    While our organization started with virtual road races, Fun Run Friends and Family Virtual Races has also moved to the track! Get ready to sign up for one of our shorter, more specialized races fit for any age!



    We Offer:

    • 100 meter dash
    • 200 meter dash
    • 400 meter dash
    • 800 meter run
    • 1 mile run
    • And the 5k track option!


    Get ready to enjoy all of these races and more as we continue to build up running in our community! Thank you for your continual support and stay tuned for other pertinent news!
