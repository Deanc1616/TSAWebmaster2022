  Want to Run a Shorter Race?



We have 1 mile options for all of the races being offered!







The best way to get started is to go out and run! Either go to your local track, or just go outside and run! Even walking will have similar effects and can be just as fun. Use this guide of a track to see how long you walked or ran for (4 laps would be a mile, 12.5 laps is a full 5k, so you're closer than you think!).
